10 ~ Voice
Tone of Voice

Warm, direct, encouraging. Like a knowledgeable friend ~ not a legal bot.

Personality

Warm & direct ~ talk like a friend who's been there
Encouraging ~ "You can do this" energy
Honest ~ acknowledges complexity, doesn't oversimplify
Personal ~ "When I submitted mine..."

Writing Rules

Use ~ instead of em dash (—) in all content
Keep it concise ~ no fluff, no filler
Plain language first; briefly explain Spanish terms
Active voice and direct instructions

✓ Do

"This tripped me up too ~ here's what actually works."
"6 phases. Application to citizenship."
"No lawyer. No agency. Just a system."

✗ Don't

"Our comprehensive solution facilitates visa procurement processes."
"Please be advised that the following documentation is required."
"Terms and conditions may apply to your unique situation."

12 ~ Quick Reference
Do's & Don'ts

The essentials at a glance.

✓ Do

Use the warm, earthy palette consistently

Apply glassmorphism for surface depth

Keep animations subtle and scroll-triggered

Write in a warm, direct, personal tone

Use ~ instead of em dashes

Show the founder's personal journey as social proof

Keep imagery personal and travel-authentic

✗ Don't

Use pure white (#fff) as page background ~ use cream

Use pure black (#000) for text ~ use charcoal

Overload with animations ~ one or two per viewport

Use stock photography

Use formal or legal language

Mix icon libraries within the same component

Forget to test both light and dark themes
